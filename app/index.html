Appeal of the founder

In the vision of each Kyrgyzstan, which feeds hope for the best, Kyrgyzstan by 2030 will become a country of competitive, with a diversified economy, high-income per capita, full and active member of the global community. To achieve this, it is necessary to focus on the main wealth - human resources. The driving force of the economy, the enlightened elite is scientists, teachers, investors, entrepreneurs and workers who create knowledge, finance and develop a business, provide the economy by the workforce and create the competitiveness foundation at the global level.
